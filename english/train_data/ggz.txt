Houkai Gakuen 2 (or Guns Girl Z) is the first online mobile game created by miHoYo (also known outside of China as HoYoverse), the creator of Honkai Impact 3 and Genshin Impact. The game website is available in Chinese and Japanese.
What is Guns Girl Z / Houkai Gakuen 2?
In a post-apocalyptic world, what would you do to survive when your school friends get turned into zombies? What would you do to save your loved ones?
Collect different weapons uniforms, badges and servants to make yourself stronger!
Beat the zombies and save your friends!
Although the apocalypse is already upon us, you still have a chance to find out the real reason behind it and stop the total destruction of humanity. Take care of yourself in this dangerous world!
Game Features
* Simple, pick-up-and-play arcade action with anime elements.
* Large amounts of unlockable content, including powerful weapons, pretty uniforms and helpful badges!
* Utilize more than 800+ weapons, 250+ uniforms and 300+ badges to create your own arsenal.
* You are not alone, there are over 15 different playable characters and over 100 different servants to select as a helper during stages.
* Playable characters feature famous Japanese and Chinese dubbing specialists.
* Gun Girl Z boasts a full OST as background music.
* Different in-game events every week.
